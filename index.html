Registered Syrian refugees from 2012 to today

Since the crisis in Syria began, millions of Syrians have registered as refugees. These refugees are registered by the UN, and then taken to safe locations. The refugees have no choice about where they will be placed. For exapmle, Marwan and his wife and children were placed in Indiana, while Marwan's brother was placed in Switzerland. More than 4 million people are now registered Syrian refugees. Source: UNCHR

Batman family path from Syria to America

Syria and LebanonThe Batman family spent their who lives in Homs, a city in Syria. Marwan owned a small restaurant and grocery, and their four kids attended school in the area. However, after the Syrian regime blew up Marwan's restaurant and the family was threatened, they fled to Tripoli in Lebanon, where they lived for roughly two years.

London, EnglandAfter the United Nations registered the Batman family as refugees, their next priority was to get out of the Middle East. They flew out of Tripoli and had thier first stop and layover in London, England.

United StatesFrom London, the Batmans arrived in Miami, Florida, and then flew into Indianapolis. When they landed, Exodus, a Christian group, had snow boots and jackets for the kids. They had never seen snow before, and had never been in a climate as cold as it was.
